// THE FOUNDER

Marketing that converts, run by the person you hired.

I'm Sean McKenzie, Director of SMC Digital. I've spent over a decade working with businesses across Australia, New Zealand and the US, managing paid ads, websites, SEO, social and email.

Before marketing, I worked in sales. That background shapes everything I build: I care about what makes someone actually buy, not what makes a report look busy. Clicks and impressions don't pay wages. Customers do.

I started SMC Digital to be different from the typical agency model. When you work with me, you work directly with me. No offshore teams, no account handovers, no junior learning on your budget; just honest advice, clear strategy and marketing built around your goals.

Away from the desk you'll find me on the golf course, deep in the latest golf tech, or having a beer at the local pub.
